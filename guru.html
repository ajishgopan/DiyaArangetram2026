About the Guru

A Life Devoted to the Art

Vidushi Rashmi Sudheendra, a dancer of exceptional brilliance and devotion, was born into a Mysore family deeply rooted in the fine arts, particularly music. She is the daughter of Sri Nagaraj and Smt Susheela Nagaraj, who initiated her into dance at the tender age of three.

An accomplished exponent of the Pandanallur style of Bharatanatyam, she underwent rigorous training for over 12 years under her revered guru Dr. Vasundara Doraswamy. She secured first place in her Bharatanatyam Vidwath program and has also completed her senior examination in Carnatic vocal music.

From a young age, Rashmi garnered numerous prestigious awards in dance. She has performed in several acclaimed dance-dramas of her guru and has earned recognition and praise from both critics and audiences alike.

Having resided in the United States for over 25 years, Rashmi continues to contribute immensely to the cultural landscape. She has taught and choreographed semi-classical and Bollywood dance across the tri-state area and has performed at venues such as TV Asia's South Asian Program, Staten Island Hindu Temple, New York Kannada Koota, Triveni, and Brindavana.

About

Founded in 2013 by Guru Smt. Rashmi Sudheendra, NRPAC is a thriving community of over 100 students in New Jersey. With classes in Hillsborough, Montgomery, Franklin Park, and Kendall Park, the school has become a cornerstone of Indian classical dance in the tri-state area.

Focusing on the Pandanallur style of Bharatanatyam, NRPAC also offers semi-classical, folk, and Bollywood dance, as well as formal SAMPADA certification in affiliation with Potti Sreeramulu Telugu University.

Our Mission

To impart the rich Indian culture and tradition through Bharatanatyam — nurturing students in a supportive environment where they develop not only artistic skills but also discipline, confidence, and a deeper appreciation for their cultural roots.
